Not to sound like the beginning of every Wikipedia page, Utsav Oza (Oz) is an engineer turned designer born, and raised in India. He has a master's in Interaction Design from Harbour.Space University.
He loves solving problems and has had a fanatical obsession with Interactive Installations for many years now. He wishes to develop his own interfacing concepts, that feel more human by using immersive technologies (Extended Realities) along with embedded hardware.
When discovered in the wild, Oz is often found geeking about science fiction, playing ridiculously old arcade games, and complaining about bad kerning in typefaces.

Design Process


User Experience Design
Ecosystem Analysis, User & StakeHolder Interviews, Card Sorting, Personas, Sitemaps, Features & Functionality Matrices, User Flows, Wireframes & Annotating like Oscar Wilde.
Tools: TBH, Mostly pen and paper
Visual Design
Art Direction Establishment (Typography & Colors), Responsive Grids, Layout Exploration, Pushing Pixels Around & Making them colors pop.
Tools: Figma & Adobe Suite
Experience Design (AR/VR/Experiential)
Storyboarding, Tech exploration, Game Mechanics, Spatial Analysis & Some Creative Coding.
Tools: Processing, Javascript & Unity
Motion Design
Storyboarding, Motion Test, Photo-Manipulation & Illustration, Keyframing till After effect crashes, Clearing Cache & Yet Keyframing again.
Tools: After Effects
