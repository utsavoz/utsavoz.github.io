Nomad
Installation
©2020

Brief

During the times of the Pandemic when everyone is shifting to working from home, Developing nations are having difficulty in transitioning online due to propaganda of data providers in those regions to create a class divide in the accessibility of data.

Design Process

Solution

In order to motivate people from developed nations to share their roll over data to developing countries, we created an experiential phone booth where participants received an interesting message from their future self.
